Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

1. Overview: The company shall provide services of securing the concerned clients/customers properties and items as agreed upon, per the contract. Basically, the company will cover vulnerable positions such as access control, screening of vehicles and personnel entering (and exiting) the premises during normal and non operational hours, monitor peoples behavior, approach within the premises.

2. Objective: The company shall be responsible to:

  • Provide security as per the terms of reference (ToR)
  • Provide number of security guards as per the contract
  • Assure safety of employees, guests and visitors
  • Assure safety and protection of clients property, vehicles, equipment’s and other materials as required
  • Respond to emergencies and provide assistance, take appropriate actions as needed, within legal limits
  • Communicate effectively in time and to the concerned authority, any directives or pertinent information
  • Act pro-actively and responsibly

3. Duties of security guards: With the fundamental objective of protection of person, protection of property and prompt response during emergencies, the security guards shall perform the following duties:

  • Access control: Allow entry to authorized personnel and vehicles only
  • Inspection: Conduct regular patrol, checks to ensure safety and security
  • Maintain: Records of people and/ vehicular movement
  • Assist: Staff / authorized personnel in times of need and as required
  • Monitor: Electronic devices (CCTV, metal detectors etc.)regularly for proper functioning
  • Reference: Keep handy names and contact numbers of concerned authorities, personnel for regular, prompt intimation
  • Inform: Any suspicious activities, including people – vehicular movement
  • Report: Commencement and completion of duty, situation and observations

4. Limits: In due course of executing their duties, security guards ought to maintain strict discipline and limitation of authority (institutional notifications of clients to be considered) as follows:

  • Identification: Cecurity guards may request for proper ID of persons or vehicles, restricting one to enter or exit the
  • Requests: Premises, however, in situation of non compliance, no force shall be used and, intimated to the concerned authority
  • Emergencies: In situation of emergencies like fire, earthquake or other unfortunate incidents, security guards may resort to means necessary for safety/security of person or property and report ASAP
  • Legal: Whilst performing his/her duties, security guards shall always act under the legal limits
  • Performance: Security guards shall, at all times, strictly perform their duties as prescribed

5. Professionalism: Security guards shall:

  • Always maintain a professional appearance and exhibit positive attitude
  • Be courteous and patient, not engage in argumentative discussions regardless of provocation
  • Not use violent, sarcastic or insolent language or gestures, and shall not express by word or deed indicating prejudice concerning race, religion, politics, gender, age or other characteristics of a person
  • Not speak to any unconcerned individual or group regarding the activities of their assigned place of work, unless authorized by concerned authority as such
  • Shall completely refrain from use of narcotic drugs or alcohol at any times, in the breach of which, one shall be subject to disciplinary action of the company and, as per the law
